Absolutely. Lusid Certify provides a closed, encrypted, SOC-2 aligned vault. We process your data exclusively to generate your compliant PDF dossier, and your financial information is never used to train public language models.

Why is there an extra fee for multiple owners?

Under federal guidelines, every disadvantaged owner whose shares contribute to the required 51% majority must submit their own individual narrative and personal financial history. Yes. The requirement for a subjective Personal Narrative stems from the federal USDOT Interim Final Rule (49 CFR Part 26). Because our architecture maps directly to the federal statute, the resulting defense dossier is universally compliant for any certifying state agency nationwide.

Compliance is not a one-time event; the state requires an annual update of your financials and recent business history.
